Hi! Welcome to PSE.

We are a group of STEM students who love programming and believe it is one of the best skill sets to have as scientists and engineers. So we made this club to serve as a community for all future Longhorn .

What We Do


Workshops

Sessions that aim to help everyone from beginners to advanced learn as much as possible. Topics cover programming for beginners, scientific computing using Python, and more.

General Meetings

Where we invite faculty and companies who use programming in their work to discuss about their projects and real life applications.

Meetups & Social Events

So we can meetup, practice, and ask questions about anything. While having fun.
